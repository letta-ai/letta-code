# Enterprise Tier Pricing - Sales FAQ

**Quick Reference Guide for Sales Team**

---

## 1. Can existing customers upgrade mid-contract?

**YES** - Existing customers can upgrade to Enterprise tier at any time during their current contract period.

- The upgrade takes effect immediately upon approval
- Pricing is prorated for the remainder of the current contract term
- New Enterprise features and support SLAs activate within 24-48 hours
- Customer will need to sign an Enterprise addendum to their existing agreement
- Contact Customer Success to coordinate the transition and ensure smooth onboarding

---

## 2. What's the difference between Professional and Enterprise support SLAs?

| Feature | Professional | Enterprise |
|---------|-------------|------------|
| **Response Time (Critical)** | 4 hours | 1 hour |
| **Response Time (High)** | 8 hours | 2 hours |
| **Response Time (Normal)** | 24 hours | 4 hours |
| **Support Hours** | Business hours (9am-5pm local) | 24/7/365 |
| **Dedicated Support Manager** | No | Yes |
| **Phone Support** | Email only | Phone, email, Slack |
| **Uptime SLA** | 99.5% | 99.9% |
| **Onboarding & Training** | Self-service | White-glove onboarding + quarterly training |

---

## 3. Are there volume discounts for seats above 500?

**YES** - Volume discounts are available for Enterprise tier customers:

- **500-999 seats:** 10% discount
- **1,000-2,499 seats:** 15% discount
- **2,500-4,999 seats:** 20% discount
- **5,000+ seats:** 25% discount (custom pricing available)

**Note:** Volume discounts apply to the total seat count and are calculated at contract signing. Contact Sales Leadership for custom enterprise deals above 5,000 seats.
